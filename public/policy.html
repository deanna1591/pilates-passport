We use your information to:

5.2 Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access – Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification – Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure – Request deletion of your data ("Right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction – Limit how we use your data
  • Portability – Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection – Object to certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: dataprivacy@pilatespassport.app

5.3 Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently delete your data within 30 days.

6. Location Data

Pilates Passport uses location data to detect nearby Pilates studios, auto-prompt you to log classes when you visit a studio, and distinguish local vs. travel classes.

You are in control: Location is only used while you use the App (not in background). You can deny location permission at any time. Manual logging is always available as a fallback.

7. Apple HealthKit Integration

If you choose to connect Apple HealthKit:

  • We only read workout data (Pilates, Functional Strength, etc.)
  • We never write data to HealthKit
  • You must explicitly grant permission
  • You can revoke access anytime in Settings
  • Health data is never shared with third parties

8. Children's Privacy

Pilates Passport is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including end-to-end encryption for sensitive data, Row Level Security (RLS) in Supabase, regular security audits, and HTTPS for all data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.
